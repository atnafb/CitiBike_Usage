About Me

Data doesn't lie. In this project, I explored the gender perspective of Citi Bike usage in New York City using publicly available data. This dashboard visualizes bike usage trends using datasets from June 2016 and 2019. I aimed to answer five key questions: rider gender proportion, changes in gender distribution over time, trip duration variations by gender, subscription preferences by gender, and the age distribution of riders by gender. The visualizations within this dashboard provide answers to these questions. This project was a valuable learning experience, allowing me to analyze and understand Citi Bike usage patterns from a gender perspective.
